Here are some perks of learning guitar

After you already know about benefits of learning an electric guitar, here are some necessary stuff you need to unleash your inner ROCK spirit!

GUITAR!

Getting an electric guitar can be frustrating and time-consuming step for someone who knows nothing about it. Don't be lazy and just pick cheapest one online. Visit a guitar store, look around, and have it try out! In case you don't know what to do; don't hesitate to ask store assistants; they are all happy to help a newby.

The price of an electric guitar can go from cheapy-cheap as $199 to luxiously expensive as $4000+. Don't make a decision based on price alone. Good beginning guitar should be the one that makes you want to keep picking it up and practice with a reasonable price.

Here is what makes me never stop practicing!

Amplifier

Amp can be so expensive as well. Don't worry to much about it. You can always upgrade later in life as you progress. Pick one that can do many in one.

For example, it can replicate many professional grade amp. Or, it has built-in paddles which could save you a lot of money from buying electric guitar effects. Or, it can connect to headphones in case you live in an apartment that has a thin wall and easily-annoyed neighbor.

Here is some idea.

Picks

Pick one that you feel comfortable holding it for a couple hours.

Here is what I've been using.

Strap

Roughly around $10-30. Pick one that's comfortable and looks good on your guitar!

Guitar Cable

There is no need for a profressional grade here. I prefer one with at least 15-20 ft. long. It's good to have a length to spare in case you want to move around the room.

Tuner

This is the cheapest, yet most important thing you need to begin your guitar journey. Believe me it'll make your life much easier.

Sources for guitar chords and tabs.

www.songsterr.com

Songsterr.com is a free source of guitar chords and tabs. It has a special feature where you can see how a song and a tab progress simultaneously.

www.ultimate-guitar.com

Like Songsterr.com, Untimate-guitar.com offers tons of chords and tabs for you to practice a song of choice. It also has an app which allows you to have an access everywhere you'd like. However, some features may require a paid-subscription for membership.
